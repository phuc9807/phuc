Fire King

Giá: 12,792,000,000 VND

Mô tả: The Fire King deck, a FIRE-attributed archetype in Yu-Gi-Oh!, revolves around a strategy of destroying its own monsters to trigger powerful effects and build advantage. Key monsters like Fire King Avatar Kirin and Sacred Fire King Garunix are crucial for setting up combos and disrupting the opponent. The deck utilizes Fire King Island and other support cards to facilitate monster destruction and search for essential pieces.
